Color Scheme Overview

For the color scheme, I wanted to go with colors that match the logo. Thr contrast of the pale yellow with the gray reminds me of a sunny day versus a cloudy day. For the fonts I chose black and white. The hover button is an orange which matches the same orange in the logo. I also chose gray for the navigation bar and lighter gray around the columns.
