Target Audience Description

I read through 2 articles which included actual survey's to deteremine who checks the weather reports and where is their go to source. There was a factor on one study that showed more women than men check weather reports daily. The largest demographic being the 45-59 age group. Of this age group, approximately 56% were women.
The 2nd report determined that the 20-35 age group and 60+ age group was the largest 2 groups that consistenly checked weather reports. While the groups in both studies represented different age demographics, the findings of where they got their news reports remained the same.
Both studies determined that the most popular place to check the weather report was a default weather app. The second sourse, is the local TV news. This came as a surpise to the writers running the study. The third is a specific website or app and the fourth is The Weather Channel. I based my personas and scenarios off of these studies.

Two Personas

Mariah: - 27 year old female
- Works as a graphic designer for an advertising firm
- Single
- Travels frequently for work and leisure
Mary Ann: - 50 year old female
- Works for as a lawyer
- Has 2 children
- Involved in community events and local organizatios

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenario One: Mariah is a bridesmaid at her friends wedding in Chicago in September. She will need to find a reliable weather site to prepare her outfits for the 5 day trip. Scenario Two: Mary Ann is spearheading a charity 5K event in April. She will need to know the weather predictions in order to be prepared with supplies and set-up of tents or covered areas. Scenario Three: Mariah is in putting together a social event for local businesses they collaborate with. She will need to know what type of weather to plan for since the theme is a Hawaiian Luau and will need to be outdoors. Scenario Four: Mary Ann is taking a trip with her husband to Australia for their 20th annivesary. This will be in Melbourne where there's the possibility to experience all 4 seasons in 1 day. Scenario Five: Mary Ann will be going to a conference in Chicago. She will be joined by 3 other colleagues. Though the convention will be mostly indoors, the weather can be unpredictable when they are outdoors.
