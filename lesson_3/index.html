Site Name

The Site Name is skychecknow.com.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to become the resource of up to date weather information, updates, and alerts. Anyone planning on vacation to a particular destination can browse the site to be better prepared for the upcoming weather for that time frame. Locals to the area can see latest emergency alerts or warnings and can share to social media pages. Also they can sign up to receive weekly updates of the forecast for the next week and sign up for text alerts regarding emergency updates.

Available Domain Name

I looked through the recommended google domainsite and found the available domain name of "Sky Check Now." Its short, describes what the site's function will be, easy to remember and the word "Now" has a sense of being the most up to date weather report. The cost for the domain is $12/month.
