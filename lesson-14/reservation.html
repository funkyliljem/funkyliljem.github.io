Reservation Info & Packages


Package Information
Length: Skill Level: Cost:
4 hours Level I & II $125/person
5 hours Level II & III $170/person
6-7 hours Level III & IV $220/person

What we provide:

We provide lifevests, paddles and helmets. Safety training for each person.

What you bring:

Make sure to wear or bring dri-fit type or water repeling clothing and watershoes. You will need to change into this clothing before you board the raft. Gloves also recommended.

Additional items for purchase:

Lockers are provided onsite for a nominal fee. Lunch can be provided for a $7.00 fee per person if requested. Water bottles available for purchase.
