Welcome to Salmon River Adventures

We are the premeire river rafting company in Riggins, Id. Our family owned company employees many family members and long-time rafters. We train to help you have fun in a safe environment. You have fun and we will show you how it's done! We specialize with first time or inexperienced rafters. Group and family rafting experiences are also available and can be customized depending on the riders familiarity of rafting. You can count on our experience to keep you at ease while giving you the white water adventure of a lifetime!



Rafting Adventures

For the Beginners

The first adventure is perfect for beginners. We encourage those with minors 12 and over as well as large groups with this option. We spend the first 30 minutes going over safety protocols. We also ensure that all rafters know the route and commands that will be asked of them to do as we paddle down the river. The parts of the Salmon River we will explore will be mostly Level I & II. The tour also consists of a lunch break and is 4 hours long. We stop off will be at Lakeside Bunglows for the break for 1 hour. Then continue onto Riggins. The total time rafting will be approximately 2 hours.


For the Intermediate

Our second package is perfect for those who have some experince in rafting or water sports. The first 30 minutes will go over the safety protocols and test your skill level. We want to ensure that you have some knowledge of basic swimming and water safety. After which we will set down to the Salmon River into the Level II & Level III areas. The whole tour will consist of 5 hours with stop half way through for a 1 hour break at Lakeside Bungalows. Then continues on to the final desination in Riggins. Total time rafting is about 3 hours.


For the Advanced

Our Advanced package is for the experienced rafters. We highly encourage that only skilled rafters choose this package. You will need to present your certifications to get into this group. We start off with a safety demonstration and certify your documents. This package will includes over 10 miles of river and will take about 6-7 hours to complete with a break at Lakeside Bungalows about 6 miles into the tour. Total time rafting is about 5 hours.
