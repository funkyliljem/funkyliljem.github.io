Welcome to Salmon River Adventures

We are the premeire river rafting company in Riggins, Id. Our family owned company employees many family members and long-time rafters. We train to help you have fun in a safe environment. You have fun and we will show you how it's done! We specialize with first time or inexperienced rafters. Group and family rafting experiences are also available and can be customized depending on the riders familiarity of rafting. You can count on our experience to keep you at ease while giving you the white water adventure of a lifetime!
