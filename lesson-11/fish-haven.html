Lakeside Subdivsion Being Built

By Fish Haven News

Fish Haven Lakeside Subdivision is being built to accomodate the growing community. This peaceful town is home to beautiful lakeside views and is a summer home for nearby towns of Preston and Soda Springs. The growth seen comes no surprise to long-time resident, Priscila Martin. She says, "The small time charm of Fish Haven is also the beauty of it. Where your neighbors are not only friends but more like family. We welcome the need for new subdivisions like Lakeside to accomodate the growing need."

Construction is set to take place soon as the finalization of the project gets approval from the community planning department, which could take place in a couple weeks. Once it is set, families can begin the process of reserving their lots and choosing pre-approved home plans for quick building. More information will be confirmed in the next few weeks once final approval is publicly announced.
