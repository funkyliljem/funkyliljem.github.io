Soda Springs National Park Brings New Jobs

By Soda Springs News

The Soda Srings Geyser is now a part of the newly minted Soda Springs National Park. This will bring crowds over the upcoming summer season. With the new changes there will be lots of new hiring required to ensure a safe and successful camping season. Check out the hiring event this coming Saturday, January 24th from 11a-5pm and bring your resumes and come ready for an interview.

The positions that are being filled vary from park rangers, store attendants, and guides. This is also a perfect position for high school students wanting to get first hand experience with working for the National Park Service. This opportunity also includes the ability to earn some college credits for college elective courses. If you'd like more information on the possibility of joining our National Park Service group, contact National Park Director James Smith at (757) 891-7354 for more information or on the web at: https://nps.soda-springs.hiring.gov.
